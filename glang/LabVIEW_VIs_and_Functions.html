VI and Function Reference

The VIs and functions located on the Functions palette depend on the visible palette categories. Click the Customize button on the Functions palette and select Change Visible Palettes from the shortcut menu to display all palette categories on the Functions palette. You also can click the Change Visible Palettes link at the bottom of a pinned Functions palette to display the palette categories.

The VIs and functions on the Functions palette can return general or specific LabVIEW error codes.

(Windows) To view related topics, click the Locate button, shown at left, in the toolbar at the top of this window. The LabVIEW Help highlights this topic in the Contents tab so you can navigate the related topics.
