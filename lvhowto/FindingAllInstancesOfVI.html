Finding All Instances of a VI or Type Definition

Complete the following steps to find all instances of a VI or type definition.

  1. Right-click a VI or a type definition on the block diagram or in the VI Hierarchy window. You also can right-click the VI icon or connector pane in the upper right corner of the front panel or block diagram. (Statechart) Right-click the icon in the upper right corner of the Statechart Editor window.
  2. Select Find All Instances from the shortcut menu. LabVIEW displays the results in the Search Results window.

You can replace some or all instances of the VI or type definitions. You also can find and replace block diagram objects or text.
