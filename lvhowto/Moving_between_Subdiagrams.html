Moving between Subdiagrams

Use one of the following ways to scroll through the available subdiagrams:

  • Click the decrement and increment arrows in the structure selector label, if available.
  • (Windows) Move the cursor over the selector label and press the <Ctrl> key while moving the mouse wheel. (Linux) Move the cursor over the selector label and press the <Meta> key.

Use one of the following ways to move to a specific subdiagram:

  • Click the down arrow next to the case or frame name and select the subdiagram from the shortcut menu.
  • Right-click the structure border and select Show Case or Show Frame. Select the case or frame label from the shortcut menu.
