Passing Data through Frames of a Flat Sequence Structure

If you use a Flat Sequence structure, the data from outside the sequence structure enters the frame as each frame executes. The data leaves the frame after the frame executes.

Complete the following steps to pass data from one frame to any subsequent frame in a Flat Sequence structure.

  1. Select the terminal you want to wire to the Flat Sequence structure.
  2. Wire from the tunnel of one frame to any other frame in the structure. You can wire through a frame or out of one frame and into another frame.
