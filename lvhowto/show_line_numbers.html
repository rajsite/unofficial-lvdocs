Showing and Hiding Line Numbers in Script Nodes

You can show and hide line numbers in the Formula Node, the MATLAB script node, and the (MathScript RT Module) MathScript Node. The MathScript Node shows line numbers by default.

Complete the following steps to show or hide line numbers in a script node.

  1. Right-click inside the node.
  2. Select Visible Itemsï¿½Line Numbers from the shortcut menu.
