Inserting Block Diagram Objects

Complete the following steps to insert an object into a wired block diagram.

  1. Right-click the wire at the position where you want to insert the object.
Tip  To connect the wires to the most appropriate terminals at the bottom of the object, right-click the wire itself or directly above the wire. To connect the wires to the most appropriate terminals at the top of the object, right-click directly below the wire.
  1. Select Insert from the shortcut menu to display the Functions palette.
  2. Select a VI or function on the Functions palette to insert the new object on the wire. If the number of terminals or data types in the objects are different, you might have broken wires.
