Graphing Digital Data

Complete the following steps to display a digital signal in a digital waveform graph.

  1. Acquire a digital signal or convert data into digital data.
  2. Add a digital waveform graph to the front panel.

     Add  Find
  3. On the block diagram, wire the resulting digital data output or digital waveform output to the digital waveform graph terminal.
  4. Run the VI.

Refer to the Create Digital Waveform VI in the labview\examples\Controls and Indicators\Graphs and Charts\Digital Waveform Graph directory for an example of graphing digital data.
