Displaying Terminals for VIs, Functions, and Express VIs

Display the connector pane terminals for a subVI, function, or Express VI to make wiring easier.

Note  If you display a subVI or Express VI as expandable, you cannot display the terminals for that node.

Complete the following steps to display the terminals for a subVI, function, or Express VI.

  1. Right-click the VI or function.
  2. Select Visible Items»Terminals from the shortcut menu.

To return to the icon, right-click the VI or function and select Visible Items»Terminals from the shortcut menu to remove the checkmark next to the Terminals shortcut menu item.
