Removing Structures without Deleting Objects in the Structure

Complete the following steps to remove a structure without deleting the objects in the structure.

  1. Right-click the structure you want to remove.
  2. Select Remove Name of Structure from the shortcut menu.

With For Loops and While Loops, LabVIEW copies the contents of the loop to the block diagram and automatically connects any elements wired through tunnels.

Removing a structure with multiple cases preserves only the visible case, frame, or subdiagram and any block diagram terminals. All other cases, frames or subdiagrams are deleted.
