Organizing Palette Categories

You can change the category order in the Category (Standard) and Category (Icons and Text) formats. On a pinned palette, use the following methods to change the order in which categories appear on the palettes:

  • Right-click a category and select Move this Category Up or Move this Category Down from the shortcut menu.
  • Click the double lines to the left of a category and drag the arrow to where you want the category to appear.
  • Right-click a category and select Move to Top (Expand by Default) from the shortcut menu to move the category to the top of the palette. By default, LabVIEW expands the top-most category.
