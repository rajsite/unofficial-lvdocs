Designing Wideband FIR Filters

You can use the interpolated finite impulse response (IFIR) technique to produce wideband FIR lowpass filters and wideband FIR highpass filters. A wideband FIR lowpass filter has a cut-off frequency near the Nyquist frequency. A wideband FIR highpass filter has a cut-off frequency near zero.
