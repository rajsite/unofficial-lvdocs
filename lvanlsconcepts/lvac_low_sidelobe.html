Low Sidelobe

The Low Sidelobe window reduces the level of the side lobe at the cost of broadening the main lobe. The following equation defines the Low Sidelobe window.

for n = 0, 1, …, N - 1

where N is the length of the window
ω = 2πn/N
a0 = 0.471492057
a2 = 0.17553428
a3 = 0.028497078
a4 = 0.001261367
